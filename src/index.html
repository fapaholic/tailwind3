Serie ｢ゼーリエ Zērie?｣ is an elven mage known for her expansive knowledge in the area of magic. She is described as a living grimoire and is the head of the Continental Magic Association. Serie refers to herself as a Great Mage from the Mythical Era.

Heiter

Heiter ｢ハイター Haitā?｣ was the alcohol-loving priest of the Hero Party. After the Hero Party was dissolved, he returned to the Holy City of Strahl and eventually became a bishop. Heiter had very good health.

Himmel

Himmel ｢ヒンメル Hinmeru?｣ was the Hero of the Hero Party. With the help of Priest Heiter, Mage Frieren, and Warrior Eisen, he defeated the Demon King, creating an era of peace. After his death from old age, making him the catalyst for the main storyline.

Fern

Fern ｢フェルン Ferun?｣ is a young human mage, and she accompanies Frieren as her apprentice and party member. She is an orphaned war refugee originally from the Southern Lands, later adopted by Heiter and placed under Frieren's care after his death.
